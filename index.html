About Me

I am a Swiss Student from EPFL (École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne, in english: Federal polytechnic School of Lausanne). I am currently doing a one year exchange at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. I will graduate in Spring 2019 in Computer Science, with a minor in Data Science.

Apart from this technical formation, I am passionate about design and typography. I designed a few websites and created some paper documents. I am conversational with most web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, TypeScript, etc.). For paper, I love LaTeX (a programmable typesetting system) which I used for the paper documents showcased below.

Design Process

When designing, I try to be target focused. First I want to have a design adapted to the task at hand. Second I want the design to be beautiful and consistent so that it makes the target want to read or explore.

I love web design and lots I the thing I design tend to have a flat or material inspired design.
